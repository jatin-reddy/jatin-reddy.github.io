I am a Graphic Design Volunteer at a student-led non-profit volunteer organisation called TheGoodOrg.

What do we do? :

  1. Spread awareness about socio-political, cultural, economic issues.
  2. Aid the underprivileged.
  3. Protect the environment.

My Primary role is to design aesthetically pleasing and concise web pages, digital posters, reports, advertisements, and other communication materials at TheGoodOrg.
